ADMO IS A DIGITAL SIGANGE FRAMEWORK THAT LETS YOU RAPIDLY BUILD INTERACTIVE GESTURE APPS

Admo is a powerful framework for building and managing gesture apps using Microsoft Kinect. The framework is designed for digital signage and uses industry standard technologies and best practises. We've used the framework to build dozens of apps and completed tests with thousands of real shoppers.

Designed for digital signage

Analytics

Learn how many people are using Admo, what products your customers are interested in, the conversation rate of passersby and more – all from Admo’s online analytics dashboard.

Content Management

Admo makes it super simple to keep content up to date. When you want to update images or copy you can simply upload new files to Dropbox and it will automatically update.

Remote Monitoring

Admo notifies you if a player is having any problems so you can avoid those nasty Windows error messages. You can also get screenshots to ensure the on-screen content is always right.

Scalability

Admo is the first gesture platform designed to handle rollouts of 1000's of players.

Industry Standard

We built Admo from the ground up to be compatable with industry-standard digital signage technology. You get to build gesture apps in HTML5!

Works Offline

Admo works offline. If the internet drops Admo will keep on being awesome.

Modular

Admo is composed of a number of modular components - making it easy to integrate with your existing digital signage technology.
